Critical gaps — fix these first
Structural / technical
Critical
No sitemap.xml found
Google cannot reliably discover all 8 blog posts without a sitemap. With a new domain, this is the #1 reason posts don't get indexed.
Generate sitemap.xml and submit to Google Search Console immediately
No Article schema (structured data)
None of the blog posts have JSON-LD schema markup. Google can't show rich results (author, date, reading time) in search — you're invisible in featured snippets.
Add Article + BreadcrumbList schema to every post
Zero backlinks to the blog
cybiqon.in is a new domain. Without any external sites linking to your blog posts, Google has no trust signal — posts may never rank regardless of content quality.
1 quality backlink from an indexed site can trigger crawling of all posts
Blog posts not in Google Search Console yet
Only the case-studies page appeared in search results — individual blog posts are either not indexed yet or not being crawled.
Use URL Inspection in GSC → "Request Indexing" for every post URL
On-page SEO
High priority
Meta descriptions missing or auto-generated
The page source shows no hand-written meta description tags. Google auto-generates them, which hurts click-through rate in search results.
Write a 140–155 char meta description per post with the target keyword
H1 is duplicated on every post
Each post renders the same headline twice — once as a subheader tag and again as an H1 inside the content body. Google sees two H1s which dilutes authority.
Keep only one H1 per page — remove the duplicate in the content body
No internal links between posts
Posts don't link to each other. This means Google's crawler hits a dead end after reading one post — it doesn't discover the rest of the blog naturally.
Add 2–3 contextual internal links per post to related articles
Author page missing
Posts show "Cybiqon Team" as the author but link to no author bio page. Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) requires real author credentials to rank well.
Create an author bio page with your name, photo, and expertise. Link from every post.
Images have no alt text
The LinkedIn-style OG images used as hero images have no alt attributes visible in page source — missed opportunity for image SEO and accessibility.
Add descriptive alt text to all blog images — include the target keyword
Content gaps
Medium priority
No keyword research visible in post structure
Posts target broad phrases like "WhatsApp automation for Indian MSMEs" but not the long-tail questions people actually type — e.g. "how to set up WhatsApp bot for small business India free".
Use Google's "People Also Ask" + Ahrefs free tools to find low-competition long-tails
No FAQ section in posts
FAQ sections with schema markup are one of the fastest ways to appear in Google's "People Also Ask" boxes — especially for question-based MSME searches.
Add 3–5 FAQs at the end of each post with FAQPage schema
Category/tag pages not optimised
The blog index page has tag filters (MSME, India, automation) but the tag pages themselves likely have thin/no content — Google may see them as low-quality duplicate pages.
Either add intro copy to tag pages or noindex them to avoid diluting crawl budget
